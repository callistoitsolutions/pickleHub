About This Product

Our Homemade Mango Pickle is crafted with love using the finest raw Alphonso mangoes sourced directly from Konkan farms. Every jar carries the authentic taste of Nagpur's rich culinary heritage.


Ingredients

  • Raw Alphonso Mangoes (60%)
  • Cold-pressed Mustard Oil
  • Rock Salt, Turmeric, Red Chilli Powder
  • Fenugreek Seeds, Fennel, Nigella Seeds
  • Asafoetida (Hing) — No artificial preservatives

How to Use

  • Serve as a side dish with dal-rice, roti, or paratha
  • Use as a spread on sandwiches or bread
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Always use a dry spoon to serve
  • Refrigerate after opening for longer freshness
